About IT Knowledge Exchange

IT Knowledge Exchange (IKE) is about spreading knowledge based in information technology.
Every developer is interesting - and therefor trying to become an expert - in a slightly different field of IT. Thats why we are trying to set up meetings once a month to get others interested in the different fields.
Usually we have three presentations each 20 minutes + 10 minutes discussion / questions time.
